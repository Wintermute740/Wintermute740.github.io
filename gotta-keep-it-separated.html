Gotta Keep It Separated

February 06, 2025 — Wintermute

Most people who know me personally don't know this, but I have been separated from my wife on-and-off for the past few years. I posted to FB when it happened but that upset my wife, so I deleted the post. I was hoping to work things out, but that looks less and less likely. We're still best friends, and are probably better for each other separately than as a couple. We're co-parenting well, and all is fine, which is why I never told anyone else since that initial post. If this is how you're finding out, I'm sorry for not telling you sooner. I just don't care to talk about it because I am fine and don't want it to be what people focus on when they're around me.
