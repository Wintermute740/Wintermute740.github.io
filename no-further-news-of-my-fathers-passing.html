No further news of my father's passing

February 05, 2025 — Wintermute

A few weeks ago, I posted about my father's passing on January 14. Since then, I have heard absolutely nothing. I find it peculiar that I still cannot find his obituary or notice of his funeral or anything else about it online. I expected to at least see a post on Facebook by the half-sister who notified me, but I have not even seen that. I find this rather... peculiar...
